about dgtlJar

IT Consulting

dgtlJar is an Athens-based IT consultancy focused on SAP, DevOps, and Blockchain. We work closely with our clients to deliver practical solutions that make a real difference.

SAP Development:

We build SAP solutions around your actual workflows — from implementation to long-term support.

DevOps:

From CI/CD pipelines and container orchestration to cloud infrastructure and observability, we help engineering teams move faster without sacrificing stability. Whether you're modernising legacy systems or building from scratch, we bring the tooling and practices to make it work.

Enterprise Blockchain Consulting:

We specialise in enterprise blockchain — primarily Hyperledger Fabric, Hyperledger Besu, and Consensys Quorum — covering architecture, smart contract development, and integration with existing business systems. We also work across other enterprise-grade platforms depending on your needs.
